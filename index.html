What is astrology?

Astrology is an ancient art that dates back over 6000 years ago. Through witnessed movement of the planets and stars, archetypes are born, as this linked the great dance of the Gods with human affairs. Astrology is not purely a digital print of a chart on a screen or paper, but rather a living, 3D experience of the cosmos.


With the Hermetic quote, "As above, so below," there is an interconnectedness with all beings. As we have an opportunity to witness the magical development of human consciousness, this Above and Below bridges the gap between heaven and earth.


Patterns of the Cosmos will play out, and it's our own willingness to choose to participate in the dance which will create a particular ecstasy within our being when "heaven gains another messenger."


Come join me on a journey of Soul development and self-discovery. Let's awaken the great mystery within and re-member our essential nature!
