Poetry

Comes with a price

All gifts and all treasures
All troubles and all failures
All come with a price
All love and sacrifice
All disdain and all surmise
All come with a price
Believing you are free
Without consequence
Will surprise you with its coming return
Ignorance and bliss
Acceptance of the true
Either way will require its toll
Lost or found
Gone or present
Inevitably creating the terms and conditions
Signed on the dotted line
All comes at a price
No interest
No additional service fees
But you will reap what you sow
No matter what you played with the roll of the dice
Never forget, all comes with a price
