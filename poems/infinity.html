Poetry

Infinity

Plaster on these walls a million faces
Hollowing through thousands of places
Leaving marks on the surface of the skin
Reminding the power held within
The craze has become addicted for more
Breaking the skin weaker than before
Scarring the eyes melting the mind
For ourselves we've lost never to find
Sweeping the dirt under the rug
Catches our heart with a pull to tug
Losing the ring that once was there
Hiding the pain for which we dare not bear
As I walk this path with eyes and smiles
Past the people for many miles
I see colors never seen before
Taking my hand and placing in on a door
More and more the doors appear
To see many faces now filled with fear
Further down and with my surprise
I see faces with anger in their eyes
For days it seems I talk to walls
Hearing their triumphs and hearing their falls
I take one step and I seem to remember
I've walked from January until December
Back to January I seem to see
The beginning of the path which started with me
I stare at the face next to another
I stare at the faces of me and my brother
And my sister and father and mother and friends
And I've realized this from beginning to end
Life is a surprise with many faces
We travel the world to many places
And still these walls which we've seen before
All link together through every door
We all come from the same source
From some infinite unknown force
As I leave this place with my spirit held high
I remember the fact in which I cannot die
These people are a part of me
As I just as much as a part of thee
We together build this home
A burning desire and never alone
All the feeling we experience through life
Can be wonderful or a painful strife
So as I disappear into the wind
One last voice is spoken within
I always was and will always be
I am forever, My name, Infinity
