The journey

Many of the people I associate with have a dedication to self-growth and spirituality. Often times these folks worked in corporate jobs and had no sense of 'awakening'. Their stories kind of go like this: they had some sort of crisis in their life, left their jobs, and went on some sort of spiritual/healing journey.

My journey is a little of the same, but slightly different. I grew up in a town named Arlington, WA. It is a relatively traditional white American environment. In 2009 I had my own 'awakening' and moved to Seattle. From there, I went to massage school, and by the time I graduated, I was involved in several different spiritual paths and healing modalities. These included reiki, plant medicine, shamanism, astrology, tantra, and finally a path about humaning.

A few years ago I had a crisis in my life that forced me to leave the massage industry and wander for a bit. I am a passionate person, so I wanted to find more of my passion. I began to really dive deep into astrology, and found my whole Universe opened up, literally. Along side that was a growing cultivation of my mental capabilities, so I also decided to embark on a journey to learn programming. Clearly I love language!

I'm not a conventional person. I think and see the world in many different ways compared to the mass majority of people. It doesn't make it easy to relate with everyone. But when I do find the kinds of people to talk with, we share many amazing insights, teachings, and have a very deep heart and soul connection. It's moments like those that I live for.

It is my hopes and intentions that I share a positive impact on the world, encouraging people to gain new perspectives, discovering their truth, and living from an open hearted place.
